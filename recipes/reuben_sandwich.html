Reuben Sandwich

Description

A reuben is a grilled sandwich consisting of corned beef on rye bread. It is commonly found as a menu item in Jewish style delicatessens. Its a delicious combination of meat and cheese.

Traditionally, a reuben consists of thinly slice corned beef, Swiss cheese, and sauerkraut on rye bread. It is then slathered with typically Russian or Thousand Island dressing.
